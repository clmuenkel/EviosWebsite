# Phase 5: Integrations + Offer

**Goal:** Integrations section (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, etc.) and Offer / Risk Reversal section with exact copy and "Book a demo" CTA. Static.

**Context:** Design brief §7 (Integrations), §8 (Offer). Dark, clean.

---

## Prerequisites

- Phases 1–4 done.

---

## Tasks

- [ ] **Integrations section** (id `integrations` for nav):
  - Headline: **Keep your stack. We integrate into it.**
  - Copy: "Already using software? Perfect. Evios overlays your workflow and pushes booked jobs + notes where you need them."
  - Integration tiles (text or simple SVG badges): ServiceTitan | Jobber | Housecall Pro | Google Calendar | Zapier / Webhooks
  - Microcopy: "If you don't use any software, we can still book into a shared calendar and send job details by text/email."
- [ ] **Offer / Risk Reversal section** (id `offer` for nav):
  - Headline: **Try it for 30 days. If it doesn't create value, pay $0.**
  - Offer list:
    - Free discovery (we map your intake + booking rules)
    - Free implementation (we build + integrate)
    - Free usage for ~30 days
    - If you don't want it after, you pay $0
  - CTA button: **Book a demo** → scroll to `#contact`
- [ ] Styling: dark, clean; tiles readable; list clear.
- [ ] Mobile: tiles and list wrap/stack; CTA tappable.
